name: Move Card Across All Columns
---

Navigate to the kanban board at http://localhost:3000. Find the card titled "Research competitors" in the "To Do" column. Click the "Move Right" button on that card and assert that it is now in the "In Progress" column. Then, on the same card in "In Progress", click the "Move Right" button again. Assert that the card titled "Research competitors" is no longer visible in the "In Progress" column and is now visible in the "Done" column.
