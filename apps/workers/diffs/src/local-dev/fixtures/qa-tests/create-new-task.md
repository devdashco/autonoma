---
name: Create New Task
---

Navigate to the kanban board at http://localhost:3000. Click the "New Task" button in the top-right corner of the header. In the "Create New Task" dialog that opens, type "Write blog post" into the "Task Title" field, type "Draft the Q2 product launch announcement" into the "Description" field, and type "Morgan Smith" into the "Assignee" field. Open the "Priority" select and choose "High". Click the "Create Task" button. Assert that a new card titled "Write blog post" is visible in the "To Do" column with assignee "Morgan Smith" and a "High" priority badge.
