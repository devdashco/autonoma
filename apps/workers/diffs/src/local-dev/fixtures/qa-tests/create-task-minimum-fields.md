name: Create Task With Minimum Fields
---

Navigate to the kanban board at http://localhost:3000. Click the "New Task" button in the top-right corner of the header. In the "Create New Task" dialog, type "Quick task" into the "Task Title" field and type "Taylor Rivera" into the "Assignee" field. Leave the "Description" empty and leave the "Priority" at its default value. Click the "Create Task" button. Assert that a new card titled "Quick task" is visible in the "To Do" column with assignee "Taylor Rivera" and a "Medium" priority badge.
